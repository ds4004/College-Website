Documents necessary

For JEE Students

  1. Personal documents :
    • Adhar card of student and parents
    • Birth Certificate of student
    • Leaving Certificate of student
    • 10th result copy
    • 12th result copy
    • Bank account details
    • Category Certificate (if applicable)
    • PwD Certificate (if applicable)
    • OCI Certificate or PIO Card (if applicable)
  2. Documents by Jossa and NTA :
    • Provisional JoSAA Seat Allotment Letter of that year
    • Passport size photographs similar to one uploaded in JEE Main 2021 application form
    • Candidate Undertaking
    • Proof of Seat Acceptance Fee Payment by e-challan or Internet Banking through SBI
    • JEE Main Score Card 2021
    • JEE Main Admit Card 2021
    • JEE Advance Score Card 2021
    • JEE Advance Admit Card 2021

For DASA Students

  1. Personal documents :
    • Birth Certificate of student
    • Leaving Certificate/Migration Certificate of student
    • 10th result copy
    • 12th result copy
    • Bank account details
    • Category Certificate (if applicable)
    • PwD Certificate (if applicable)
    • OCI Certificate or PIO Card (if applicable)
    • NRI Certificate
    • Transfer Certificate from school
      • (Dasa students need to pay fee in dollars)
  2. Documents by Jossa and NTA :
    • Provisional JoSAA Seat Allotment Letter of that year
    • Passport size photographs similar to one uploaded in JEE Main 2021 application form
    • Candidate Undertaking
    • Proof of Seat Acceptance Fee Payment by e-challan or Internet Banking through SBI
    • JEE Main Score Card 2021
    • JEE Main Admit Card 2021
    • JEE Advance Score Card 2021
    • JEE Advance Admit Card 2021
